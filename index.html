A charged marble drop (0.197 s lag, 5.598 m/s² vs. 9.8 m/s²) offers evidence, hinting at applications in propulsion, time control, and consciousness-physics bridges. This paper targets the cold dark matter crisis (cusp-core problem) while addressing 21 unsolved problems (see Annex).
